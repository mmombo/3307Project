Processes cart for issues, (not enough funds, not enough stock).

If no issues are found, checkouts items in cart by removing their quantity from stock and subtracts the total price from the provided member's balance.

Parameters

productCProductCollection to apply changes to.
histCPurchaseHistoryCollection that records cart upon succesful purchase.
Returns
0 on failure, 1 or higher on success.
